How It Works

Three simple steps

No instructions needed — the puzzle teaches itself.

1

See the target

Each puzzle shows you a target number. Your goal is to arrange numbers so the equation equals that target.

2

Place your numbers

Tap or drag number tiles into the blank slots. Each number can only be used once — think before you place!

3

Solve & score

Match the target to complete the puzzle. Earn coins, build streaks, and climb the difficulty ladder.
