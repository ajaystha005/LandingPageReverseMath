Privacy Policy

How we handle your data — simply and transparently.

Last updated: January 2, 2026

Quick summary: Reverse Math Puzzle does not require an account, does not collect your name or email, and stores all game data locally on your device. We use anonymised analytics and may show ads. We do not sell your data.

1. Who We Are

Reverse Math Puzzle ("we", "our", or "us") is an independent mobile game available on iOS and Android. Questions about this policy can be directed to support@reversemathpuzzle.com.

2. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information

We do not collect any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or physical address. You can play the game without creating an account or providing any personal data.

b. Gameplay & Usage Data

We may collect anonymous, non-identifiable usage data to improve the app, including:

  • Game progress, scores, and session duration
  • Streak counts and daily challenge participation
  • Feature usage (which screens you visit, which power-ups you use)
  • Device type, operating system version, and app version
  • Crash reports and performance data

This data is anonymised and cannot be used to identify you personally.

c. Advertising

The app may display optional advertisements (for example, watching an ad to earn extra coins). Our advertising partners may collect limited data to deliver and measure ads, such as a device advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS, GAID on Android) and ad interaction signals. You can opt out of personalised ads in your device settings.

Purchasing the ad-free upgrade removes all advertisements. If you have opted out of personalised advertising at the device level, only contextual ads will be shown.

d. In-App Purchases

If you make an in-app purchase (such as the ad-free upgrade or coin packs), the transaction is processed entirely by Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not receive, store, or have access to your payment information.

e. Push Notifications

If you enable the optional daily reminder, the app will request permission to send push notifications on your device. Notification preferences can be changed at any time in your device Settings app. We do not use notification tokens for any purpose beyond sending you the local daily reminder.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to:

  • Improve gameplay balance and puzzle difficulty
  • Fix bugs and improve app stability
  • Understand how players discover and use features
  • Deliver and measure optional advertisements

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

The app integrates the following third-party services, each subject to their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Google) — Analytics and crash reporting
  • RevenueCat — In-app purchase management
  • AdMob (Google) — Advertising (optional ads only)
  • Sentry — Error monitoring and crash reporting

These services may collect anonymised device and usage data as described in their respective privacy policies. We do not share personally identifiable information with any of these services.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • All game data (scores, streaks, settings) is stored locally on your device.
  • No game progress is stored on external servers.
  • We take reasonable technical measures to protect any data in transit.
  • Deleting the app removes all local game data from your device.

6. Children's Privacy

Reverse Math Puzzle does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app contains math-based content suitable for all ages, but any in-app purchases require parental approval on accounts for users under 18. If you believe a child has submitted personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

7. Your Rights & Choices

You can:

  • Use the app without providing any personal information
  • Opt out of personalised ads via your device's advertising settings
  • Disable push notifications in your device Settings
  • Purchase the ad-free upgrade to remove all advertising
  • Delete the app at any time to remove all local data

For residents of certain jurisdictions (e.g., the EU/EEA, California), additional rights may apply. Contact us at support@reversemathpuzzle.com for any data-related requests.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: support@reversemathpuzzle.com
  • Website: www.reversemathpuzzle.com
