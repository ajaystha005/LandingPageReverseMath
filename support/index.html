Frequently Asked Questions

Each puzzle shows you a target number and a partially filled equation with blank slots. Your job is to tap or drag number tiles into the blanks so that the equation equals the target. Remember: multiplication and division are calculated before addition and subtraction (standard order of operations), and each tile can only be used once.

Classic Mode — Solve as many puzzles as possible in 3 minutes. Earn points for each correct answer.

Challenge Mode — Complete a set of 10 puzzles as quickly as you can. Use power-ups strategically.

Daily Challenge — One fresh puzzle every day. Complete it to maintain your daily streak and earn coin rewards.

There are four difficulty levels:

🌱 Novice — Simple equations to learn the basics
📚 Seasoned — Moderate equations for daily practice
⚡ Expert — Complex equations for fast thinkers
🧠 Genius — Maximum difficulty, hardcore mode

The Daily Challenge uses the Seasoned difficulty by default.

Coins are earned by completing Classic and Challenge Mode games, maintaining daily streaks, and watching optional rewarded ads. Coins are spent on power-ups during gameplay: Hint (reveals one correct placement), Skip (skips a puzzle), Undo (undoes your last move), and Add Time (adds 30 seconds in Classic Mode). You can also purchase coin packs from the Coin Store in the app.

💡 Pro Tips

🔢

Order of operations matters

× and ÷ are calculated before + and −. Plan your placement with the final result in mind, not left-to-right.

💾

Save coins for tough spots

Don't spend hints on easy puzzles. Hold them for Expert or Genius difficulty when you're really stuck.

📅

Daily first, then ranked

Do the Daily Challenge before anything else — it's only available for 24 hours and keeps your streak alive.

↩️

Undo is free — use it

Not sure? Place a number and see if the equation gets closer. If not, undo immediately and try another tile.
