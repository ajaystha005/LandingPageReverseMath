Privacy Policy

Reverse Math Puzzle

Last updated: January 2, 2026

Reverse Math Puzzle (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Reverse Math Puzzle mobile application (“the App”).

Quick summary:
  • No account required.
  • We don’t collect your name, email, phone number, or address.
  • We may collect anonymous usage data to improve the app.
  • Ads may use limited device identifiers for ad delivery/measurement.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information

We do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or address. You can use the App without creating an account.

b. App Usage Data

We may collect non-personal, anonymous data to improve the App, including game progress and scores, streak counts and rewards, app interactions (such as screens viewed and features used), device type, and operating system information. This data cannot be used to identify you personally.

c. Advertising Data

The App may display advertisements. Ads may be optional (for example, watching an ad to earn extra rewards). Advertising partners may collect limited information such as device identifiers and ad interaction data to deliver ads and measure performance.

d. Purchases

If you choose to make in-app purchases (such as removing ads), payments are processed by the App Store or Google Play. We do not store or receive your payment information.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected data to:

  • Improve gameplay experience
  • Balance difficulty and rewards
  • Fix bugs and improve performance
  • Understand how players use the App

We do not sell, rent, or trade your data.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Game data may be stored locally on your device and/or in the App’s databases.
  • We take reasonable measures to protect data from unauthorized access.
  • No system is completely secure, but we follow industry best practices.

4. Children’s Privacy

Reverse Math Puzzle does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will remove it.

5. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services for analytics, advertising, and performance monitoring. These services may collect anonymous data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

6. Your Choices

You can:

  • Use the App without sharing personal information
  • Disable ads by purchasing an ad-free option
  • Stop using the App at any time

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted with an updated revision date.
